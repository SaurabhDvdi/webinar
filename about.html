About Almanet

Empowering individuals and organisations with practical, industry-grade IT training and trusted technology services.

Who We Are

Almanet Professional Services delivers practical IT solutions and industry-focused training. We combine years of enterprise delivery experience with hands-on education to help learners and organisations succeed.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Equip students and early-career professionals with job-ready technical skills through project-driven learning, mentorship, and real-world workflows.

  • Outcome-first curriculum design
  • Industry projects and reviews
  • Career guidance and interview prep

Our Vision

Be a trusted bridge between industry and talent—delivering workforce-ready training and reliable technology services.

  • Strong industry collaborations
  • Scalable programs and tooling
  • Continuous upskilling ecosystem

Our Learning Approach

Hands-on Projects

Leadership

Yeshwant Maheshram – Founder

Yeshwant Maheshram is a seasoned IT and business executive with over 25 years of global experience in SAP implementation and cloud migration. He has led multiple large-scale SAP S/4HANA transformations, helping organizations optimize operations and accelerate digital transformation.

He has held leadership roles at SAP India, Deloitte Consulting (Chicago), EDS, TCS, HighBar Technocrats, and Cybertech Systems, working with clients across the USA, UK, UAE, and India.

Yeshwant holds an M.E. from IISc Bangalore, a B.E. from SGSITS Indore, conducted research at IIT Delhi, and earned a Diploma in Business Administration from Washington, DC.
